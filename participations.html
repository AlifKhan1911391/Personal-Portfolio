About BDAIO 2026

The Bangladesh Artificial Intelligence Olympiad (BDAIO) is a national-level competition that tests participants on core AI, machine learning, data science, and ethical reasoning. I secured Top‑2 at the Regional round (Dhaka) among 380+ competitors, advancing to the National stage and ranking in the Top‑7 across Bangladesh — one of the highest student recognitions in the country's AI landscape.
