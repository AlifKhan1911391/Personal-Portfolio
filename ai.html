  • Repository: Zenodo
  • DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18913179
  • Also indexed on: OpenAIRE

2. Spring-Powered V8 Engine Model: Mechanical Design, Energy Conversion, and Thermodynamic Analysis

  • Published: March 23, 2026
  • Repository: engrXiv
  • DOI: 10.31224/6657
  • Summary: Presents a theoretical mechanical design of a hand-loaded, spring-powered V8 engine model. The machine converts manually pre-stored elastic potential energy (governed by Hooke's Law) into sustained rotational mechanical output through a six-stage energy conversion pathway. Analytical estimates suggest approximately 40% useful mechanical output, accounting for bearing friction and aerodynamic losses. Provides a technical framework for future STEM demonstrators.
  • Keywords: Spring Engine, V8 Model, Mechanical Design, Energy Conversion, Hooke's Law, Thermodynamics, STEM
