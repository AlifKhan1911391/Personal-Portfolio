Entrepreneurship Journey

Poedagar 613 | E-Commerce Venture

In May 2025, I launched my first entrepreneurial venture, Poedagar 613, with just 3,000 BDT (about $25 according to the conversion at that time). It was a manual dropshipping operation where I collected watches and accessories from Daraz and marketed them for sale on Facebook. Although the business didn't generate sales, it provided invaluable real-world lessons in product sourcing, digital marketing, customer acquisition, and the mechanics of bootstrapped e-commerce.
